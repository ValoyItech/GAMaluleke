Our Services

Road Accident Fund Claims

We assist clients in securing rightful compensation for injuries, damages, and losses resulting from road accidents. Our team handles all aspects of RAF claims, from initial documentation to court representation if necessary.

Administration of Estates

Our probate attorneys guide families through the complex process of estate administration, including asset verification, debt settlement, and legal transfer of inheritance.

Personal Injury & Medical Negligence Claims

We advocate for victims of personal injury and medical negligence, helping them receive compensation for medical expenses, lost income, and emotional trauma.

Unlawful Eviction Claims

Our housing law specialists defend tenants' rights against unlawful evictions, ensuring proper legal processes are followed and providing representation in housing tribunals.

Divorce Matters

Our family law attorneys handle divorce proceedings with sensitivity and expertise, ensuring fair settlements for assets, child custody, and spousal support.

Drafting of Wills & Legal Agreements

Our estate planning team creates comprehensive wills, trusts, and legal agreements tailored to your specific needs, ensuring your wishes are protected.

Criminal Matters

Our criminal defense attorneys provide vigorous representation for various criminal charges, ensuring your rights are protected throughout the legal process.

Land Claims

We assist clients in navigating land claims processes, providing expert legal guidance for property disputes and historical land restitution matters.

Labour Matters

Our labour law department advises both employers and employees on workplace disputes, unfair dismissals, and compliance with employment legislation.
